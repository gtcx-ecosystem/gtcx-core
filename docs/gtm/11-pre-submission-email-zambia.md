## Email Draft

Subject: **Request for Pre-Submission Consultation — Digital Verification for Zambia's Mining Sector**

Dear Regulatory Sandbox Team,

I am writing to request a pre-submission consultation regarding the GTCX Verification Protocol — a cryptographic infrastructure for digital verification of commodity origins and trade compliance, with specific application to Zambia's copper and cobalt supply chains.

### What We Do

GTCX provides cryptographic verification infrastructure that:

- Creates **tamper-evident digital certificates** for commodity lots at point of extraction
- Tracks **chain of custody** from artisanal mining cooperatives through aggregation and export
- Enables **offline verification** — critical for remote mining sites on the Copperbelt and Northwestern Province
- Supports **compliance evidence** for international due diligence requirements (EU Conflict Minerals Regulation, OECD Due Diligence Guidance)

### Why Zambia

Zambia's mining reform agenda aligns directly with our capabilities:

- **Copper** (world's 7th largest producer) and **cobalt** supply chains require digital traceability as international buyers demand verified sourcing
- **Artisanal and small-scale mining (ASM)** formalization — our identity credential system provides digital IDs for producers, cooperatives, and aggregators entering the formal economy
- **Emerald provenance** — Kagem mine and surrounding small-scale operations benefit from origin verification
- The Bank of Zambia's commitment to fintech innovation through the Regulatory Sandbox provides an appropriate controlled environment for validation

### Our Readiness

- Internal cryptographic security assessment with 9.9 million fuzz test executions, zero vulnerabilities
- FIPS 140-3 compliance via validated cryptographic modules
- ISO 27001, SOC 2, GDPR, PCI-DSS, SOX compliance documentation
- 21 CI quality gates enforced on every code change
- All cryptographic operations use internationally audited libraries — no custom implementations

### Our Request

We would welcome a 30-minute consultation to:

1. Understand admission requirements for the Regulatory Sandbox
2. Clarify whether internal security assessments are accepted
3. Discuss a pilot scope — we propose copper supply chain verification from ASM cooperatives to first point of export
4. Understand timelines and reporting requirements

### About GTCX

GTCX builds verification infrastructure for commodity trade in Africa. We do not custody funds, process payments, or handle personal data. Our platform is open-source, offline-capable, and built for the operating conditions of Zambia's mining regions.
