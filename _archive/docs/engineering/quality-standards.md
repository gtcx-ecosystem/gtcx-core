# Quality Standards

This is the canonical summary of the 10/10 quality standard for gtcx-core.

## Principles

- Every feature has tests, evidence, and traceability.
- Architectural decisions are documented via ADRs.
- Performance and security budgets are enforced and tracked.

## Required Artifacts

- Remediation tracker and audit report
- Evidence logs (UAT and quality gates)
- Performance budgets and benchmark history
