# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in our project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our standards

**Positive behavior:**

- Welcoming and inclusive language
- Respect for differing viewpoints
- Graceful acceptance of constructive criticism
- Focus on what is best for the community and the product

**Unacceptable behavior:**

- Harassment, trolling, or personal attacks
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Other conduct inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Report concerns to the repository maintainers via the contact in [`SECURITY.md`](./SECURITY.md). All complaints are reviewed promptly and fairly.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in all project spaces and when an individual officially represents the project in public.
